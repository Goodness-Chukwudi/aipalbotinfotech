Many people wonder, how is it possible for people to connect and engage comfortably without any distance limitation? You might have taught the same as well, thinking about the modern innovations going on in the world and considering where we were many years ago. The modern technological revolution is driven by what we called Cloud.

Cloud is simply the "New Internet", it is the means of unlimited vast data storage and unbounded processing power. It runs over the internet to expand the capability and possibility of innovations on the web. The cloud is a collection of various solutions from different vendors. Many big conglomerate companies provide data centres to other people to use, and thereby charge very cheap amount for the sharing model of physical data centres across the world. Such companies are Amazon e-commerce (AWS Cloud), which has become the leader in the modern cloud revolutions, followed by Microsoft (Azure Cloud), and also Alphabet (Google parent, GCP). There are many other cloud providers still working towards better innovation.

It is very important that corporate organizations, government and individuals begin to look towards building their future applications in the cloud, leveraging the cheap payment model, designs, innovations, production-ready services that cloud providers offer.
