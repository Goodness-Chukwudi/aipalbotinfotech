IT Trainings

Aipalbot provides different formats for our students depending on their schedules. We offer on-site training, our offices across the countries are available with stable electricity (supported by standby generator) and very fast internet access which students can connect to.

We also offer Live Virtual Training, this helps our students to connect from any location, locally or internationally to attend their classes, they have the opportunity to experience the same professional hands-on technical training irrespective of their present location.

Our training is in categories, we also offer career advice to help anyone trying to choose a career in IT and ICT. We offer Cloud Technologies Training in a hybrid model, from our experience, we noticed that having the knowledge of the cloud technology without knowing how to use it to build applications won't be enough to empower people to be self-employed or self-sufficient.
